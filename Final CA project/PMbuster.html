About

Digi-Deals is a company dedicated to bringing our customers' favorite video game accessories to the real world!
Using the latest in dimensional-crossing technology, us here at Digi-Deals do our best to bring you quality products.
While we cannot guarantee that every product will look exactly the same as they do in their respective worlds,
thanks to our retrieval technology, we CAN guarantee that they will work exactly the same as you remember!
(And for those kind hearts out there, don't worry! These products were not stolen from their owners;
we were given their spares with their permission!)
